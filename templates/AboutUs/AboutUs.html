About Us

Want to establish a system (Driver Drowsiness Detection System) that is used to reduce road fatalities. The system uses the camera that detects the drivers' closure period of eyes using facial landmarks and detect the level of drowsiness of the driver by image processing. The system observes the movement of the eyes of driver within the sequence of the image, and on drowsiness , an alarm gets activated to alert the drivers and save them from any kind of accident. In addition to that a voice chatbot (Voice to voice) is integrated so that when the driver feels drowsy, they can turn on the chatbot and start a talk in order to get back to normal state.
